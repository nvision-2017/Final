About


nvision, IIT Hyderabad in asscoiation with ABLab solutions presents to you a workshop on Arduino based Internet Of Things.

Attend the workshop and learn how to control household appliances wirelessly from anywhere in the world using Wi-Fi technology.

In this workshop you'll learn the basics of embedded systems, digital electronics and you'll be introduced to the Arduino platform and many more.

There's going to be practical sessions where you'll get to test out all the things you've learned on your own!

*No pre requisites required




Venue: IIT Hyderabad

Date: 7th & 8th January

Timing: 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Registration Fee: ₹ 1250

Workshop Benefits & Highlights

  • Build & develop your own Wi-Fi controlled wireless home appliances system
  • Gain conceptual knowledge on Embedded System, Embedded C++, Arduino, Relay, Wi-Fi etc.

  • Awarded certificate on “IoT Duino-Smart Home”
  • Live Demos and Interactive Question & Answer sessions
  • Learn
    • How to use Arduino-Sketch software tool
    • How to write program for Arduino
    • How to interface LED with Arduino
    • How to interface 16X2 Alphanumeric LCD with Arduino
    • How to drive a relay with Arduino
    • How to control home appliances wirelessly from an Android Mobile or Laptop using Wi-Fi Technology
