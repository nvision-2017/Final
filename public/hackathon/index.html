This is η-hack.

η-hack is a free 36 hour hackathon, organised by ηvision - IIT Hyderabad's techfest. Our dream has always been to organise the best tech events in Hyderabad, and we plan on living upto that dream. With that in mind, we are launching η-Hack, a pure technology hackathon. This is a hackathon where you use the latest and the greatest tools to build demoes that make the audience go "WOW!"

The hackathon spans across the third weekend of December - the 17th and 18th - at Collab House, near Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad.The best hacks in each domain will recieve prizes!

We invite everyone to apply, but we have limited space. We can't accomodate everyone, even though we want to. Hence, we encourage you to indicate your areas of interest and previous work in the field while applying.

Prizes

The best hack in each theme will win a cash prize of ₹10,000!

FAQ

How does registration work?

Anyone can apply to us via the form linked above. We will contact you after the selection process. If you are accepted, you will have a week to confirm your attendance.

Does this cost money?

No! The hackathon is fully free and includes meals and snacks.

How will I get there?

They hackathon is at Collab House, near Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad. I'm pretty sure it won't be hard finding your way there :)

What about teams?

Teams can have a maximum of 4 people. You can select teammates when you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates.

What if I don't have a team?

That's alright! We'll team you up with someone you can work with on the spot.

What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

Feel free to contact any of the people listed here regarding any more doubts that you may have.
