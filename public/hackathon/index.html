RealityHack


ηvision, IIT Hyderabad presents to you a hackathon on Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality.

RealityHack is a free 36-hour hackathon set to take place during ηvision, Hyderabad's largest techno-management fest.

The aim of RealityHack is to push the envelope in the field of AR/VR. As long as you know how to code, you are welcome to join us! Stand a chance to win from a prizepool of ₹30,000

We invite everyone to apply, but we have limited space. We can't accomodate everyone, even though we want to. Hence, we encourage you to indicate your areas of interest and previous work in the field while applying.





Venue: IIT Hyderabad

Date: 21st & 22nd January

Timing: 10:00 AM, Saturday through to 5:00 PM, Sunday
