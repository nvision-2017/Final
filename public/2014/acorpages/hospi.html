INTRODUCTION

We assure you that your short stay with us will be fun, secure and comfortable. Our team will be available round the clock to make you feel at home to address your every needs

ACCOMMODATION

Limited accommodation is available during our fest.
Please mail to utkarsh@nvision.org.in in advance and specify the duration of your stay (kindly mention the dates also) at IIT Hyderabad. Mention your contact number in the mail.
You will get a confirmation mail once your accommodation is finalised. Kindly bring the printout of confirmation mail while coming to the fest.
Accommodation charges - Rs. 250/night.
Food coupons will be provided to you at the accommodation desk and payment will be taken accordingly. Rooms are provided solely on sharing basis.
Mattresses, pillows, buckets and mugs will be provided once accommodation is confirmed.
Accommodation will be provided solely on first come first serve basis.
