PAPER PRESENTATION

Introduction

It’s time to think “out of the box”. Let the budding Einstein inside you take over, fire up your grey matter, and show the world what you are capable of. We provide you the base on which one can show the world what scientific advances we have achieved. Paper Presentation contest attracts some of the best minds from all over the country. Participants are invited to present papers spanning various research topics pertaining to the different branches of engineering and science.

Event Format and Rules

Round 1:
Online submission of synopsis.
The Synopsis must contain:

• Abstract
• Problem definition
• Solution of the problem defined
• Results and discussion
• Practical application
• Summary

• Last date of submission of synopsis is 15th October, 2014.

• Participants are required to send their submissions at me10b017@iith.ac.in via email, the subject should be Paper Presentation - ηvision 2014.

• Abstracts must be in pdf format only.
• The synopsis should not contain more than 5 pages.
• Text font should be Times New Roman, point size 12, single spacing and all headings of point size 14.
• Maximum no. of team-mates is 4.
• In the mail please mention contact details of all the team members (email id and phone no.).
• Any form of plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
Those whose papers have been selected for the final round shall be informed by email within a day of the deadline.

Round 2:
• Teams selected on the basis of the first round will have to give their presentation in ηvision 2014.
• Those who are selected for the final round are required to send us a mail at me10b017@iith.ac.in by 7 days of declaration of the result, the subject should be Paper Presentation - ηvision 2014. The mail should consist of the following things :
• Acknowledgement that you are aware of you being selected.
• Details of all the team members: Names, Departments, Phone numbers, College, etc. Please try to put them in a proper tabular format for our convenience.
