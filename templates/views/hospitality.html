We assure you that your short stay with us will be fun, secure and comfortable. Our team will be available round the clock to make you feel at home to address your every needs.
  • Limited accommodation is available during our fest.
  • You will get a confirmation mail once your accommodation is finalised. Kindly bring the printout of confirmation mail while coming to the fest.
  • Accommodation charges - INR 300/night.
  • Food coupons will be provided to you at the accommodation desk and payment will be taken accordingly. Rooms are provided solely on sharing basis.
  • Mattresses, bedsheets and pillows will be provided once accommodation is confirmed. This amount doesn’t include any food charges. It is inclusive of internal transport to hostels for accommodation.
  • Accommodation will be provided solely on first come first serve basis.
  • Safety deposit of INR 200 has to be paid at the registration desk. It would be reimbursed at the time of check out.
  • In case you don’t get accommodation due to unavailability, you can always make arrangements for your stay outside the IITH campus.
    • We will be having security rooms in order to provide security to all your belongings
    Dear All,

    This is to inform you about the rules to be followed in the IITH campus, to make ηvision 2k17 a hassle- free and fun filled experience for everyone.
  • Kindly carry your college ID card and Registration card (provided during on-spot registeration) in the campus.
  • Any kind of misbehaviour will not be tolerated and will be dealt with, strictly.
  • Frisking is done for security reasons. Kindly cooperate.
  • Security personnel are not responsible for any of your personal belongings.
  • Security personnel reserve the right to refuse your entry at any point of time if they deem it unsafe.
  • Kindly cooperate with ηvision 2k17 team.
  • The accommodation fee & caution deposit may be paid at the time of registration, caution deposit will be refunded at the time of check out if the registration card is submitted back.
  • The students are advised not to keep large amounts of cash or valuables in the room. The student is responsible for the safety of his/her belongings. A security room will be present for such concerning issues.
  • Any damage/breakage to hostel property will be charged to the occupants of the room/ block with a fine. Disciplinary action will also be initiated.
